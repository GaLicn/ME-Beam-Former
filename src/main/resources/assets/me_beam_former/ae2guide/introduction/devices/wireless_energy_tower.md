A wireless energy relay.

## Linking

- Use <ItemLink id="me_beam_former:laser_binding_tool" /> to bind towers to towers.
- You can also bind a tower to a machine that has an energy capability.

## Range

- Horizontal range: within 20 blocks (X/Z).
- Vertical range: within 256 blocks (Y).
